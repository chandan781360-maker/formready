Online forms for government services, job applications, admissions, and KYC processes often reject uploads due to strict requirements. Incorrect image size, wrong dimensions, background issues, or file size limits are the most common reasons for rejection. FormReady is built to solve these exact problems.

Why Online Forms Reject Photos, Signatures, and Documents

Most portals enforce automated validation. Even a small mismatch can result in upload failure. Common rejection reasons include:

  • Photo or signature size exceeding allowed KB
  • Incorrect height and width
  • Background not white or transparent
  • Signature not properly cropped or horizontal
  • PDF file size outside allowed limits

How FormReady Helps in Real-World Scenarios

FormReady is designed specifically for real form submission needs. Whether you are applying for a government exam, uploading documents for bank verification, or submitting job application forms, FormReady helps prepare files exactly as required.

  • Resize passport and ID photos to exact dimensions
  • Prepare signatures with transparent or white background
  • Compress PDFs to 100KB, 200KB, or 500KB
  • Increase file size when minimum KB is required
  • Convert images to PDF and PDFs to images
