About FormReady

FormReady is a simple online utility designed to help users prepare photos, signatures, and documents exactly as required for online forms.

Many government portals, job applications, admission forms, and banking services reject uploads due to incorrect file size, dimensions, format, or background. FormReady solves this problem by providing precise resizing, compression, and format conversion tools in one place.

Our goal is to make form submissions stress-free by ensuring files meet real-world portal requirements before upload.

FormReady works directly in your browser and does not require any software installation.

Note: FormReady is an independent utility and is not affiliated with any government authority.
